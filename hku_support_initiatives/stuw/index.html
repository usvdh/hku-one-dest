Studenten welzijn (Stuw) - student wellbeing organisation from HKU. Stuw organises a variety of events and tools for student wellbeing:

  • Sessions with small groups of students about specefic themes such as identity, stress.
  • Workshops and trainings, such as the Mind and Body programme
  • Stuw Podcast
  • Weekly free meditation sessions online

among other things.
